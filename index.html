How to Build Your Own Space Ship: The Science of Personal Space Travel

See all formats and editions

Private space flight has long been a science-fiction dream. Today it is on the verge of realisation. Major entrepreneurs, such as the founders of Virgin, Microsoft, and Amazon, are investing in a new generation of lightweight, efficient spacecraft; start-up companies specialising in space tourism and spaceport development are burgeoning; and NASA and American aviation authorities are altering decades-old legislation to make private space adventure possible. How To Build Your Own Spaceship tells you how to join the new space race, where the sky is no longer the limit.
